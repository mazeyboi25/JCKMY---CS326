# Cost-Benefit Analysis

## Development Cost

Estimated hours:
- Frontend development: 20 hours
- Backend development: 15 hours
- Testing & debugging: 10 hours

Total hours: 45 hours  
Hourly rate: $20/hour  

**Total Development Cost = $900**

---

## Operational Cost (Monthly)

- Hosting (Vercel): $0 (free tier)
- Maintenance: 5 hours/month × $20 = $100

**Total Monthly Cost = $100**

---

## Tangible Benefits

- Faster item listing and browsing
- Reduced time to connect buyers and sellers
- Increased efficiency in campus marketplace transactions

---

## Intangible Benefits

- Improved user experience
- Increased student engagement
- Convenience and accessibility
- Better organization of campus buying/selling

---

## ROI (Return on Investment)

Assume value generated = $200/month

Annual value = $2400  
Annual cost = $900 + ($100 × 12) = $2100  

**ROI = (2400 - 2100) / 2100 = 14.3%**

---

## Recommendation

The project provides moderate financial return and strong intangible benefits such as improved user experience and convenience. It is recommended to continue development and improve features to increase user adoption and long-term value.
